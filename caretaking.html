Compassionate Care for Those in Need

World Path Care Taking Foundation is dedicated to supporting orphans and underprivileged communities through essential resources, education, and emotional support.

World Path Care Taking Foundation

Supporting orphans and underprivileged communities in need.

World Path Foundation is not only focused on educational empowerment but also extends its support to orphans and underprivileged communities through our Care Taking Foundation subgroup. We believe that every individual deserves opportunities for growth and development, regardless of their circumstances.

Through our initiatives, we provide essential resources, educational support, and mentorship to those in need, helping to create smiles on people's faces and fostering hope for a brighter future.

Our Care Taking Foundation focuses on providing food, clothing, educational materials, and emotional support to orphans and vulnerable children. We work with local communities to identify those most in need and develop sustainable programs that create lasting impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our organization forward.

Our Mission

To provide essential support, resources, and opportunities to orphans, the needy, and underprivileged communities in Ghana, helping them overcome challenges and build better futures.

We achieve this through sustainable programs that address immediate needs like food and clothing while also providing educational support, emotional care, and mentorship to create lasting positive change.

Our Vision

To create a Ghana where every orphan and underprivileged individual has access to basic necessities, education, and support systems that empower them to break cycles of poverty and become positive contributors to their communities.

We envision communities where no child goes to bed hungry, where every orphan receives love and care, and where the most vulnerable among us are supported with dignity and compassion.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Showing genuine care and empathy for those in need, especially orphans and the underprivileged.

Dignity

Respecting the inherent worth of every individual we serve, regardless of their circumstances.

Inclusivity

Ensuring our support reaches all who need it, without discrimination or bias.

Sustainability

Creating programs that provide long-term solutions rather than temporary relief.

Community

Working together with local communities to identify needs and develop appropriate solutions.

Hope

Bringing light and optimism to those facing difficult circumstances and challenges.

Our Impact

Supporting communities and helping those in need.
