About World Path Foundation

A forward-thinking organization dedicated to empowering students and transforming educational opportunities.

Welcome to WorldPath Foundation, a forward-thinking organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged students and transforming educational opportunities.

Our team of dedicated professionals specializes in providing scholarships, mentorship, and resources to help aspiring scholars achieve their academic and career goals while helping the needy/homeless with food stuffs and any other helpful resources.

We are passionate about fostering a supportive community that promotes access to education and personal growth.

By harnessing the power of collaboration and innovation, we strive to create pathways that enable students to thrive in a competitive world and make a meaningful impact in their communities.

WorldPath Group Traveling Consult

Your trusted partner for all travel needs and international adventures.

WorldPath Group Traveling Consult is a travel agency that specializes in helping people whose dream is to travel abroad. We provide comprehensive travel solutions including Aviation Services, Domestic flight services, flight booking, travel planning, holiday packages, and hotel bookings.

Established in 2025, we have helped over 50 Ghanaians to obtain VISAs to study their dream courses/programs, enjoy holidays, visit family, and work in desired countries abroad.

Our experienced team understands the complexities of international travel and works diligently to make your journey smooth and memorable. Whether you're traveling for education, leisure, or work, we're here to make it happen.

World Path Care Taking Foundation

Supporting orphans and underprivileged communities in need.

World Path Foundation is not only focused on educational empowerment but also extends its support to orphans and underprivileged communities through our Care Taking Foundation subgroup. We believe that every individual deserves opportunities for growth and development, regardless of their circumstances.

Through our initiatives, we provide essential resources, educational support, and mentorship to those in need, helping to create smiles on people's faces and fostering hope for a brighter future.

Our Care Taking Foundation focuses on providing food, clothing, educational materials, and emotional support to orphans and vulnerable children. We work with local communities to identify those most in need and develop sustainable programs that create lasting impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our organization forward.

Our Mission

To provide Ghanaian students with the resources, support, and opportunities they need to achieve their academic and professional goals, and to cultivate a generation of global leaders who are committed to serving their communities and making a positive impact on the world.

We will achieve this by offering scholarships opportunities, mentorship, and guidance, and by fostering partnerships with international universities and organizations.

Our Vision

To empower Ghanaian students to become global leaders and catalysts for positive change, by providing them with the opportunity to pursue world-class education, develop critical thinking skills, cultivate cultural awareness, and build strong professional networks.

Through our support, we aim to equip Ghanaian students with the tools and knowledge they need to address the complex challenges facing their communities and the world, and to inspire them to create a more just, equitable, and sustainable future.
